音越文創 品牌故事

Music & Beyond Creative

在音越文創的世界裡，音樂是最美妙的禮物，是一段關於陪伴和無限可能的延展。


我們相信，最好的音樂不僅僅是美妙的旋律，更是一扇通向無限廣闊世界的大門，是通往豐盛人生饗宴的鑰匙。儘管我們無法親身陪伴孩子走遍這個世界的每一個角落，我們卻能以最無價的陪伴，與孩子一同走過南極的冰天雪地，感受草原上奔騰的象群掀起的沙塵，手牽手探索熱帶雨林的豐富生態……透過音樂，這一切都成為可能。陪伴，不僅是給予，也是接受與成長的過程。


音越文創的音樂專案整合團隊，能讓您透過音樂，帶著您和孩子超越時間與空間，體驗每一分每一秒所帶來的情感波動，讓每一秒的相伴都化作永恆。隨時隨地，與您一同完成陪伴孩子成長的珍貴時光。


惠鈞老師帶領音越文創那支專業而充滿創意的團隊，引導孩子萌發天生的音樂感受力，發展與生俱來的天賦。我們制定肢體律動、音樂素養、閱讀素養、鋼琴即興、幼兒觀點等多元創新的專業課程與教材，同時也在情緒管理、美感素養和性格發展等方面著手，提升孩子情緒的耐力與穩定性，養成動靜皆宜的自律性。我們秉持「慢慢走也走得很好」的理念，與孩子一步步探索生命的每一個角落。音樂走出音越的孩子，將是每一個人生舞台上最有自信的主角。


在廣袤深邃的音樂世界裡，音越文創的專業團隊，溫柔而堅定地與您並肩前行。讓音樂引領父母與孩子，共同探索生命的意義。願我們共同創造美好的旋律，讓音樂成為陪伴我們的永恆之音。



In the world of Music & Beyond Creative, music is the most wonderful gift, an extension of companionship and infinite possibilities.


We believe that the best music is not just a beautiful melody but a gateway to an infinitely vast world, the key to a rich and fulfilling life. Even though we cannot physically accompany our children to every corner of the world, we can offer the most priceless companionship, walking with them through the icy landscapes of Antarctica, feeling the dust kicked up by galloping elephants on the grasslands, and exploring the rich ecosystems of tropical rainforests hand in hand. Through music, all of this becomes possible. Companionship is not just about giving; it is also about accepting and growing.


The music project integration team at Music & Beyond Creative can take you and your children beyond the confines of time and space through music, allowing you to experience the emotional depth of each moment. Let every second of companionship become eternal as we share precious moments with you, anytime and anywhere, to support your children's growth.


Under the guidance of Hsu Hui-chun, Music & Beyond Creative's professional and innovative team helps children develop their innate musical sensitivity and natural talents. We offer diverse and creative professional courses and materials, including body rhythm, music literacy, reading literacy, piano improvisation, and early childhood perspectives. Additionally, we focus on emotional management, aesthetic literacy, and character development to enhance children's emotional resilience and stability, cultivating a balanced self-discipline. Upholding the philosophy of "walk slowly and walk well," we explore every corner of life with the children. Graduates from Music & Beyond will confidently take center stage in their life's journey.


In the vast and profound world of music, the professional team at Music & Beyond Creative walks alongside you with gentle determination. Let music guide parents and children as they explore the meaning of life together. May we create beautiful melodies together, making music our eternal companion.
