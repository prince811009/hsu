About

介紹

許惠鈞，一位橫跨古典、新世紀、爵士與世界音樂的鋼琴家、創作家和聲音設計者，她的音樂風格涵蓋了古典音樂的優雅、新世紀音樂的清新、爵士的即興以及世界音樂的多元。她的音樂簡單純淨、溫暖柔美，加上無懈可擊的演奏技巧，讓她榮獲「台灣最受歡迎的療癒系鋼琴家」美譽，同時也是即興演奏的高手和專業評審。


許惠鈞精通鋼琴與單簧管，並擅長創作、編排和製作。她多元的音樂風格、廣泛的興趣和勇於嘗試的精神，使她在表演和跨界合作中擁有豐富經驗。除了作為鋼琴演奏家，她致力於音樂教育，深耕幼兒音樂教育領域，擔任講師，並指導兒童戲劇音樂創作及錄製有聲書。許惠鈞將柔情和愛心傾注在音樂演奏與教育中，專研並培育孩子的音樂教材。她以獨特的溫柔和純粹，陪伴每一位父母和孩子，共同走上有音樂相伴的生活道路。她極為重視「身心靈健康」，並持續創作療癒大人與孩子情緒的音樂專輯。未來，許惠鈞將繼續將她的療癒魅力融入每一個音樂作品，讓充滿溫暖與純真的美妙音符，流淌到每位樂迷的心中。


歡迎您一同體驗許惠鈞的音樂魔力，讓她的音符療癒您的心靈，伴您走過每一個美好時光。



Hsu Hui-chun is a pianist, composer, and sound designer whose artistry spans classical, new age, jazz, and world music. Her music embodies the elegance of classical compositions, the freshness of new age melodies, the spontaneity of jazz, and the diversity of world music. With her pure, simple, warm, and gentle tunes, combined with impeccable technique, she has earned the title of "Taiwan's Most Popular Healing Pianist." Moreover, she is a master of improvisation and a respected professional judge.


Hsu is proficient in both piano and clarinet, excelling in composition, arrangement, and production. Her broad musical styles, wide-ranging interests, and fearless experimentation have granted her extensive experience in performance and cross-disciplinary collaborations. Beyond her role as a pianist, she is deeply committed to music education, particularly in early childhood, serving as a lecturer and guiding children's theater music creation and audiobook production.Hsu pours her tenderness and love into both her performances and educational endeavors, dedicating herself to developing musical materials for children. With her unique blend of gentleness and purity, she accompanies parents and children on a journey enriched by music. Valuing "mind-body-spirit wellness," she continually creates healing music albums for both adults and children. Looking forward, Hsu will continue to infuse her therapeutic charm into every musical piece, allowing warm and pure melodies to flow into the hearts of every music lover.


We invite you to experience the magical world of Hsu Hui-chun’s music. Let her melodies soothe your soul and accompany you through every beautiful moment of your life.
